Libation Bowl Setup
Bone (to bless & shard)
Requires 30 Prayer. Bones are blessed (free) at the Exposed Altar, then chiselled (free, +5 Crafting XP) into blessed bone shards. Each shard gives 5 XP with a jug of blessed wine, or 6 XP with a jug of blessed sunfire wine — 1 jug fills the bowl for 400 shard-sacrifices either way. Sunfire wine additionally needs 2 Sunfire splinters per jug of wine.
